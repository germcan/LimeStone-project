Grammar for LimeStone

Section 1 - general grammar

1, All variables and function action creations must be inside a VRBrea (variable area).
2, Capitalization is required for blocks with part that are capitalized, blocks with no capitals cant have capitals.
3, As of now, the only indentation is for anything under a condition.
4, At the beginning of a VRBrea (variable area) the un (French for one) if you make a second VRbrea it must be deux (French for two) and so on (trois, quatre, cinq)
5, In a string or the name of something, if you want it to have a number it must be spelt out, not the number. (spelt out is one, two, three, etc)

Section 2 - arithmetic grammar

1, on, of, in, it is important to use the correct one in a arithmetic block.
2, You must have one of the symbols at the beginning of a arithmetic block (+,-,*,/, these are they symbols. * is multiplication and / is division).

Grammar updates along side news (every three days)
